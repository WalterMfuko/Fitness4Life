Grilled Chicken & Quinoa Bowl

420 kcal Protein: 40g Carbs: 50g Fats: 12g Fiber: 8g
25 min  |  Serves 2

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (about 6 oz each)
  • 1 cup uncooked quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium zucchini, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges

Instructions

1. Cook quinoa in broth according to package instructions. Set aside.
2. Season chicken breasts with paprika, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add chicken and cook for 6-7 minutes per side until fully cooked (internal temperature of 165¬∞F).
4. In the same skillet, cook sliced zucchini and bell pepper until tender, about 5 minutes.
5. Slice chicken and arrange over quinoa with vegetables. Serve with lemon wedges.

Tips for Your Fitness Goals

  • Muscle Building: Double the chicken portion or add 1 scoop of protein powder to the quinoa.
  • Fat Loss: Use cauliflower rice instead of quinoa to lower carbs.
  • Endurance: Add 1/2 cup chickpeas for additional complex carbs.
